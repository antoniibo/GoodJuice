NEW COLLECTION

  • Apple-Chamomile

    Craving a taste of tranquility? Our Apple-Chamomile juice is the perfect blend for you. This refreshing drink combines the crisp, sweet taste of apples with calming chamomile for a delightful flavor experience. It's ideal whether you're seeking a relaxing afternoon pick-me-up or a cozy bedtime beverage. Enjoy it chilled for a burst of refreshment or warm for a soothing sip before bed.

  • Orange-Basil

    Invigorate your taste buds with our sunshine-infused Orange-Basil juice! This delightful blend bursts with the tangy sweetness of oranges and the unexpected freshness of basil. The bright citrus notes are perfectly complemented by the herbal kiss of basil, creating a truly unique and refreshing drink. Enjoy it chilled for a cool and uplifting experience, perfect for a hot summer day or a burst of energy any time of year.

  • Watermelon-Mint

    Beat the heat and refresh your senses with our Watermelon-Mint juice! This vibrant blend captures the essence of summer with juicy watermelon and a touch of cool, invigorating mint. The naturally sweet watermelon provides a delightful base, while the fresh mint leaves add a touch of complexity and a burst of cool flavor. This refreshing drink is perfect for a poolside escape or a healthy on-the-go treat. Enjoy it chilled for the ultimate thirst quencher!
