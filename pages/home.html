About Us

  • Our History

    The story of our family business began in a picturesque corner of the Kyiv region. It is here, surrounded by lush forests and endless fields, that we draw inspiration for creating our extraordinary juices. It all started with a passion. We, the founders of the company, have always loved experimenting with the gifts of nature, combining different fruits, vegetables and herbs to obtain new, unique flavors. Over time, our passion turned into a life's work. Our mission is to bring joy to people and open up new facets of taste for them. We believe that juice can be not only healthy, but also incredibly delicious.

  • Processes

    Fruits and vegetables are crushed into a homogeneous mass, and then the juice is pressed out using special equipment that preserves all the nutrients and vitamins. The juice is filtered to remove pulp and impurities, making it cleaner and more pleasant to the taste. After pasteurization at low temperatures to preserve freshness and safety, the juice is cooled and poured into sterile bottles, which are then sealed and labeled. Thanks to our careful approach to production, you can be sure that each bottle of our juice is filled with freshness, aroma and vitamins.

  • Our Team

    Behind each extraordinary juice is a team of like-minded people who are sincerely passionate about their work. We are a family business that has been passing down the secrets of making the best juices from natural ingredients for generations. Our team consists of experienced professionals who truly believe in what they do. We are proud of our knowledge and experience, which allow us to create unique juices that bring people joy and health. We are constantly learning and developing to delight our customers with new flavors and aromas. We seek inspiration in nature, experiment with different ingredients and improve our recipes. Our team is a single entity that unites love for nature, the pursuit of excellence and a sincere desire to bring joy to people.
